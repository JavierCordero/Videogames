One Line (clone)

Just like the games found in the PS4 part, in this case we have a cross-platform clone (for Android and PC) of the popular game One Line

In this case the game has been made with Unity 3D in C#. Because of this, as opposed to Switch Dash (clone) , it has many more elements such as more complex interfaces, more sophisticated algorithms, ads integration, in-app purchases ...

The game consists of solving a series of levels in which we have to go through all the boxes of a given puzzle, ordered and grouped by difficulty, without lifting our finger from the screen of our mobile phone.

It also has a "time trial" mode in which we will have to solve a level in the estimated time to be able to access a special reward (in-game currency), which will be used to buy clues within levels.
