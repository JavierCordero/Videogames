Llueve en la facultad

In this case we find the second practice or project on PS4. This is the second of the three practices, programmed in C ++ and again also cross-platform (PS4, PC).

In this case, we load a background image and on it we make appear a series of drops that imitate the effect of rain on the background image.

Because these practices are developed on a PS4 development kit, and therefore have a confidentiality agreement behind them, it is not possible to give much more information about these PS4 projects.
