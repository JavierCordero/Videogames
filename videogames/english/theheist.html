The Heist

The Heist was created by two people on our first year for a subject called Videogames Design. It was made in Construct2.

In this game, you play an antagonist, being the thief that's trying to scape. You've just stolen a bank, but when you were trying to scape, a big explosion scattered the tickets everywhere around the city. Now must collect all of them and scape the city!

Game Images

How to install?

Just download de .zip file, extract all the files in the same folder, select your OS version and double click on nw.exe to run the game!
