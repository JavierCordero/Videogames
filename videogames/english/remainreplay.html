Remain/Replay

Remain/Replay was created by two people on our first year for a subject called Videogames Design. It's a puzzle game where you discover its background while you are playing it. It was made in Construct2.

Nothing else to say about this game, you just have to play and discover it misteries by your own. It's recomended to be played with headphones.

How to install?

Just download de .zip file, extract all the files in the same folder, selec your OS version and double click on nw.exe to run the game!
