Red Brick Sky

This videogame was developed by me and other seven students. We developed it using SDL and VS 2017 for that purpose.

You are new and scared the first day of school and your only friend is a video game console. What will happen if one teacher takes it because you can't bring consoles to class? You will have to use your imagination to discover new ways of entertainment and making friends.

As this videogame was made using SDL, it may not work in all computers because some library dependencies and because it's more than 100MB I just cant upload it to my github page without having some troubles at downloading it with git lfs.
