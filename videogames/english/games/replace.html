Re-place

Videogame created for the third GameJam in which I have participated. It is made with Unity3D and all the art as well as the programming is done by my team from the GameJam.

The game stands out in the programming section due to all the possibilities it offers. First of all, we have three types of weapons, each one according to a different type of enemy and that this one will only harm enemies of its color.

Second, each of these weapons has an added ability. The dwelling adds a "Dash" to the player when pressing a certain key. Orange will heal our lives if we hit the orange targets. The blue one will generate a protective shield around us for the most difficult enemies.
