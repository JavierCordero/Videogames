SinkBiosis

This videogame is from the second GameJam I took part in with other four students. Here we made a videogame with Unity3D, making all the necesary assets on our own using Blender for that purpose.

Get in the shape of a pirate who has been charmed by a mermaid. You must capture other pirates and betray your buddys by destroying their ships in order to feed up the mermaid. You must be fast and let those pirates on the Mermaid Island's hand or she will kill you!
