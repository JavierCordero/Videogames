Felipe's Inferno

This videogame is from the first GameJam I took part in with other three students. We got the second place. Videogame made with Unity3D.

Here we've got an action, maze, roguelike game with four diferent enemies to play against and one boss each five levels.

How to install?

Just download de .zip file, extract all the files in the same folder, double click on Felipesinferno.exe file. Have fun!
