Chis

Chis is a video game created with Ogre3D for the subject of Projects 3.

In this case, as you can see at a glance in the images, the game stands out neither for its artistic section nor for its gameplay, but for the engine behind it.

The objective of this subject therefore was the realization of a videogame engine using the Ogre3D graphics engine as a base, to which physical functionalities with Bullet3D, sound with FMOD and interfaces with CEGUI were added, in addition to an architecture by components and reading of these as well as objects, positions, materials and shaders through blueprints.
