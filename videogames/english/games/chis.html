Chis

I made this video game as a summer project all on my own. It was made with Unity3D. All the art and scripts involved in the proyect were created by me using Blender and Visual Studio 2017.

IT IS NOT A FINISHED PROYECT. If anyone wants to play it, the first thing to know is that there is many work that has to be done before the proyect is finished. As it is a summer proyect, they day summer ended, I stopped working on it, that's why there are no sounds, no life bar (even though you can be killed and kill enemies), no settings...
