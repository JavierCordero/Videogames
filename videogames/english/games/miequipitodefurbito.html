Mi equipito de furbito

This project was created for the Artificial Intelligence course with Unity3D.

This project cannot be considered a game as such since in it we find two different artificial intelligences facing each other in a football match.

One of them uses a hive mind, that is, they are all controlled by a "coach" who at all times tells them what to do and how to attack or defend.

The other is an artificial intelligence whose elements act independently, that is, they behave like individual brains. In it we find an attacker, a defender and a striker, each with a specific behavior that will depend on the situation of the game.
