The Alchemist

This videogame was developed by four students on our first year as a final proyect. It was made in Unity3D.

This is a platform side-scrolling game where you're a magician who has to stop the bad guy from destroying the world.

How to install?

Just download de .zip file, extract all the files in the same folder, double click on TheAlchemist.exe file. Have fun!
