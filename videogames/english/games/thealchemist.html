The Alchemist

This video game has been developed by four students in our first year as a final project. It was done with Unity3D. It is a platform game with side scrolling in which you take on the role of a magician who, initially, only has a melee attack. Over the course of the videogame, you will unlock your other abilities which in this case are fire and ice.

The first will serve to kill enemies, while the second will be used to move platforms in the course of the second level.
It has a varied number of enemies as well as a level boss and another final boss with a map large enough to contain secret corners and some collectibles.

Note: the game currently has only one level due to the fact that there is some type of corrupt file at level 2, which prevents some textures from loading.
